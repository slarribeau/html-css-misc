I worked at Civic Resource Group back in 2015. They were focused on public transportation and the technology for improving the experience. Many cities have open APIs that let you query bus routes and schedules. Santa Monica's transit site is a great example. I used it to build this site, which displays every bus stop in Santa Monica overlaid on a Google Map. Clicking on each stop shows you when the next bus will arrive.
